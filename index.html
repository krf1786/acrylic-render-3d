Works in Chrome, Edge, Safari, or Firefox. First load downloads the in-browser engine (~16 MB, cached afterward). Print files must carry named spot/finish channels (e.g. Mirror Silver Gloss, Black Matt) — that's how matte vs. gloss is known. Prefer the command line? See cli/prep.py in the repo.
